Welcome to our coupon management system project!
This project was built throughout our course, we have put a lot of effort into it by facing and solving many problems that taught us very much.
Our project allows companies to generate coupons as part of their advertising and marketing campaigns. The system also contains registered customers, these customers can purchase coupons, the system records the coupons purchased by each customer.

The access to the system is divided into three types of clients:
1. Administrator - Has access to every action, manage the list of companies and the list of customers.
2. Company - Manage a list of coupons associated with the company.
3. Customer - Purchase coupons.
